About

Clean, fresh water

Water On Wheels provides clean fresh water right to your door, exactly when you need it. Schedule a delivery or fill a frac tank. Our service is reliable and affordable. Professionalism is of the utmost importance to us – all our drivers are timely, courteous, and fully licensed.

Services

Rent

Rent a WOW Classic Station, WOW Mini or WOW Water Table to keep guests hydrated and reduce plastic waste at your next event! With three great water station models to choose from, keep your event hydrated with the help off WOW! If you have further needs or requirements, please contact us.

Mini
WOW Mini stations provide continuous, filtered water at your event. Like all WOW stations, these unique continuous-flow stations never run out of water at your event! Modeled after the Classic WOW station, the Mini Model offers all of the features of the Classic station, but in half the size!

Medium
WOW Water Tables are ideal for smaller events such as fun fairs, picnics, and smaller community events. WOW Water Tables are also great to accompany the rental of a WOW Classic station so attendees without a bottle can drink from the taps on the table. Equipped with 1 water bottle refill faucet and 6 drinking fountain taps, the WOW Water Table is a practical solution to keep everyone hydrated!

Classic
WOW Classic water refill stations provide continuous, chilled and filtered water at your event. WOW Classic stations connect to existing municipal water sources, hose faucets or fire hydrants. WOW’s unique continuous-flow stations ensure you never run out of water at your event WOW Classic stations require a 10′x10′ space for set-up and service, feature 4 water bottle refill faucets, and are made of entirely stainless steel.
